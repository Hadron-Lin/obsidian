# 独立性
两件事情独立,则:
$$
\begin{align}
P(A)=P(A|B) \\
P(A)=\frac{P(AB)}{P(B)} \\
\implies P(AB)=P(A)\cdot P(B)
\end{align}
$$

# 多个事件的独立性
$$
\begin{align}
\begin{cases}
P(A_{i}A_{j})=P(A_{i})P(A_{j}) \\[8pt]
P(A_{i}A_{j}A_{k})=P(A_{i})P(A_{j})P(A_{k}) \\[8pt]
\cdots \\[8pt]
P(A_{1}A_{2}\cdots A_{n})=P(A_{1})P(A_{2})\cdots P(A_{n})
\end{cases} \\

\end{align}
$$
