# 定理
$$
\begin{align}
(\mathbf{a}\times \mathbf{b})\cdot(\mathbf{c}\times \mathbf{d}) \\[10pt]
=\begin{vmatrix}
\mathbf{a}\cdot \mathbf{c}&\mathbf{a}\cdot \mathbf{d} \\
\mathbf{b}\cdot \mathbf{c}&\mathbf{b}\cdot \mathbf{d}
\end{vmatrix}
\end{align}
$$
