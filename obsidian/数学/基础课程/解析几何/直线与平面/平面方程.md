设平面法向量为$[A,B,C]$,则
$$
\begin{align}
Ax+By+Cz+D=0
\end{align}
$$
由[[点乘|内积]]定义可知.
# 点到面的距离
点:$[x_{0},y_{0},z_{0}]$,取面上一点$[x',y',z']$
$$
\begin{align}
d=\frac{[A,B,C]\cdot[x_{0}-x',y_{0}-y',z_{0}-z']}{\sqrt{ A^2+B^2+C^2 }} \\[10pt]
=\frac{|Ax_{0}+By_{0}+Cz_{0}+D|}{\sqrt{ A^2+B^2+C^2 }}
\end{align}
$$
