压缩映射本质是先求出极限,然后(使用拉格朗日)证明这就是极限

![[Pasted image 20251018183800.png|500]]
容易求得
$$
\begin{align}
a=1+\sqrt{ 2 } \\
|x_{n}-a|=|\left( 2+\frac{1}{x_{n-1}}\right)-\left( 2+\frac{1}{a}  \right)| \\
=|x_{n-1}-a|\cdot|-\frac{1}{\xi^2}|\leq \frac{1}{4}\cdot|x_{n-1}-a|=\cdots
\end{align}
$$
