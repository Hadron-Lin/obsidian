# O定义

$$
若|f(x)|\leq M|g(x)|,记f(x)=O(g(x))
$$

## o定义
$$
若 \lim \frac{f(x)}{g(x)}=0,记f(x)=o[g(x)]
$$


# taylor公式的peano余项
$$
\begin{align}
f(x)=\sum_{k=0}^n \frac{f^{(n)}(x_{0})}{k!}(x-x_{0})^k+o[(x-x_{0})^n] \\
f(x)=\sum_{k=0}^n \frac{f^{(n)}(x_{0})}{k!}(x-x_{0})^k+O[(x-x_{0})^{n+1}]
\end{align}
$$
