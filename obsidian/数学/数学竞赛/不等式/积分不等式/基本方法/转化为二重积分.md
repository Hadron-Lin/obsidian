## 切比雪夫不等式
1. $f(x),g(x)$在$[a,b]$上连续.
2. 单调性相反
$$
\begin{align}
(b-a)\int_{a}^b f(x)g(x)dx\leq \left( \int _{a}^b f(x)dx\right)\cdot \left( \int _{a}^b g(x)dx \right)
\end{align}
$$
**证明**:
$$
\begin{align}
\iint f(x)g(x)dxdy-\iint f(x)g(y)dxdy \\
=\iint f(x)[g(x)-g(y)]dxdy \\
=\frac{1}{2} \cdot \iint [f(x)-f(y)]\cdot [g(x)-g(y)]dxdy \\
\leq 0
\end{align}
$$
