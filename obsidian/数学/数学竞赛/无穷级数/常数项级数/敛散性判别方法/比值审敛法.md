$$
\begin{align}
\lim_{ n \to \infty } \frac{u_{n+1}}{u_{n}}=\rho  \\
if :\rho<1\implies收敛 \\
if :\rho>1\implies发散
\end{align}
$$
>[!warning] $\rho=1$时需要特别判断








$$
\begin{align}
\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}na_{n}收敛\implies \sum_{n=1}^\infty a_{n}收敛
\end{align}
$$
