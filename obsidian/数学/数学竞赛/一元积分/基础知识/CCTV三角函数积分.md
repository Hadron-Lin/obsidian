# CCTV
$$
\begin{aligned}
C:\sec x,\csc x函数名带C\\
T: \tan x,-\cot x函数名带T\\
V:胜利,凑齐CCT\\
CT组合靠\ln
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
\int \sec x dx=\ln|\sec x+\tan x|+C\\
\int \csc x dx=\ln|\csc x-\cot x|+C\\
\int \sec^2xdx=\tan x+C\\
\int \csc^2xdx=-\cot x+C\\
\int \sec x\tan xdx=\sec x+C\\
\int \csc x\tan xdx=-\cot x+C
\end{aligned}
$$
